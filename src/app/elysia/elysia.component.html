The University of Calgary Solar Car team has continued our innovative legacy as designers of Canada’s first cruiser class car with our new car The Schulich Elysia. The Schulich Elysia started it’s design phase in 2016 and this catamaran style cruise car was completed in 2019. This design was implemented to improve battery cooling as well as increase aerodynamics. The Elysia raced in the 2019 American Solar Challenge where it took first place in the Multi Occupant Vehicle Class.
