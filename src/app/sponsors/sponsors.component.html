Support Our Team

The University of Calgary Solar Car Team builds, operates and programs the car through the kind support from our generous sponsors. You can support The University of Calgary Solar Car Team by sponsoring, donating and helping in kind donations.

Sponsor

Our team relies on kind donations of corporations, foundation and grants to build and run our program. Sponsoring our car and team includes many benefits which are listed in our sponsorship package. The University of Calgary Solar Car Team is always looking for new partners. Please contact us for more details.

Donate

All contributions help our team to maintain our goal to educate, innovate and inspire. Anyone can contribute to our success through personal donations. Monetary donations of twenty dollars or more are eligible for a tax receipt.

In Kind Donations

It is incredibly important to our team to be able to use the best technology available to us to further progress the practicality of solar cars. It would be our pleasure to test out and use your company's products, whether software, parts, tires or solar cells. Please contact us if you would like to work with our team.
